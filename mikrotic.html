    • Mikrotik Academy

    CSI is now recognized as Mikrotik Academy in the Philippines particularly in Albay (Bicol Region). Being part of Mikrotik Academy, the CSI can offer trainings for the following certifications:

    1. MikroTik Certified Routing Engineer (MTCRE)
    2. MikroTik Certified Network Associate (MTCNA)
    3. MikroTik Certified User Management Engineer (MTCUME)






    Mikrotik is a Router software and hardware manufacturing company based in Riga, Latvia. Mikrotik products are used by ISPs, individual users and companies for building data network infrastructures all around the World("Mikrotik Company Profile", n.d).

    Three(3) Faculty Members of CSI earned Mikrotik Certifications during Mikrotik Academy Trainers Bootcamp on July 22-27, 2019.
